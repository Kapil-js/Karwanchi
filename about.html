Karwanchi’s History

The Karwanchi Companies Groups are one of the most important companies that supply the local markets in all the governorates of the country with its products which became a permanent companion to the Iraqi family. This is because the products are characterized by the specifications that enabled them to reach the level of marketing excellence.

In 2007, another company, Mina Company for production of healthy water Ltd, was established with six production lines for plastic cups and with three PET lines and three production lines for producing 18 liter water bottles on an area of 20,000 square meters, with 220 employees. Karwanchi Group of Companies has participated in many international and local exhibitions and has received several awards for its participation in many events.

The company has a founding license from the Directorate General for Industrial Development of Iraq with the number 81387, and also has a health certificates numbered 36 in addition to the certificates of ISO 9001: 2008 & 22000: 2005 and Halal certi&ate.

The Karwanchi family career started in 1999, and since that date, Karwanchi Group has been able to hold leading positions as the leading I raqi companies in these products. The company began its journey with the Ta'amim Company for soft drinks in Kirkuk governorate in 1999 with two glass production lines and with a gas factory and with three PET lines with an area of 11,500 square meters. Its current is 160 employees.

In 2010, Karwanchi Company Ltd for the production of soft drinks, healthy water, juices and dairy products was established on an area of 150.000 square meters, 60.000 of which are closed areas including production lines and warehouses, 20.000 square meters for storage areas for products, and 20.000 square meters are typical gardens and green spaces and 25,000 square meters are streets and inner corridors, as well having 25.000 square meters as a vacant space for development and expansion.
